OUR MISSION

It is our mission to serve our customers through our online platform focusing on vast selection, lower pricing and simplicity.

SEARCHING AND BOOKING ON NIILANO.COM

  • Enter region (location) and a list of cars available for rent will show up.
  • Select a car brand or car make to narrow your search.
  • Choose a car of your choice and make sure you read and understand information written about the car by host before you proceed to book.
  • Book the car and make payment via the Niilano.com platform (You can make either half or full payment to book the car but full payment must be made before booking date is due). A sms and email will be sent informing you about your booking being processed.
  • Booking becomes successful after it has been approved by the host of the car and this can be between a period of 1-60 minutes. Another sms and email will be sent informing you that booking has been approved). If after this period (1-60 minutes) host does not approve booking, you will receive a refund or Niilano.com will help you find a similar car to book on the platform.

CANCELLATIONS AND REFUND

Your booking on Niilano.com may be cancelled due to the following reasons;

  1. Failure of host to respond to your booking request. When this happens, the booking cancels automatically after a period of time. (1- 60 minutes).
  2. Cancellation by host due to his or her personal reasons.
  3. Cancellation by client.

Niilano.com works very hard to reduce the rate at which cancellations occur on the platform for clients. When your booking request is cancelled, your money will be refunded to you or held temporary while Niilano.com finds you a similar car of interest. A client is at free liberty to cancel a booking 48hrs before booking date. Anything from this will attract a fee from client.
